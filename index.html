Family Rescue: 99 Nights

Day 1 Dawn Nights left: 99 Sigils: 0/3 Goal: Rescue the child and build a house
Crafting
Shovel (wood 3 + stone 2)
House Frame (wood 20 + stone 10)

How to Play

You start in a random place. Every 2 minutes the world switches from Day to Night.
At Night, creatures roam. Touching one means GAME OVER. Don't go outside!
Gather wood from trees and stone from rocks. Craft a shovel to dig underground.

Find and complete the three miniâ€‘games at the glowing shrines to earn sigils. Dig to discover the underground chamber. You need all 3 sigils to unlock the child.

After rescuing the child, craft the House Frame (wood 20 + stone 10), place it, and complete your home. Then it says THE END.

Controls

  • Player 1: Move = WASD, Interact/Use = E, Dig = F, Open Craft = C
  • Player 2: Move = Arrow Keys, Interact/Use = Right Shift, Dig = Right Ctrl
  • Pause = P (or Pause button)

Nights limit: 99. If you reach night 100 without rescuing, it's Game Over.
